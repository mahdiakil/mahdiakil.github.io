About Me

Iâ€™m a CS PhD student in the PriSec group at Karlstad University (KAU), Sweden since 2019. My research area primarily centers around cybersecurity, with a particular focus on the Internet of Things (IoT) and the development of privacy-preserving technologies.

During my research, I have focused on Vehicular Ad-Hoc Networks (VANETs), developing non-interactive privacy-preserving authentication methods. This aspect of my research utilizes advanced cryptographic techniques, including anonymous credentials and zero-knowledge proofs, to ensure user privacy while maintaining the authenticity and integrity of user attributes.

I finished my Master's degree at Sapienza Universita di Roma in 2018 specializing in Data Networks and Security writing my thesis Multi-Channel Exfiltration Techniques.

Fluent in English and Arabic with an OK understanding of Swedish and a basic level Italian, I enjoy connecting with a global audience and expanding my multicultural perspectives.
